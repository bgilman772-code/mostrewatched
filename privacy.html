Privacy Policy

Most Rewatched — effective July 15, 2026

Most Rewatched ("the App") is a personal content-publishing tool used exclusively by its operator to publish the operator's own video clips to the operator's own social media accounts. It has no users other than its operator and collects no information from the public.

1. Information the App handles

When the operator authorizes the App with a supported platform — TikTok (via TikTok's official OAuth flow) or Instagram (via Meta's official login flow) — the App receives and stores access credentials (access and refresh tokens) and basic account identifiers for the operator's own account on that platform. The App handles no data about any other person.

2. How information is stored and used

Credentials are stored locally on the operator's own machine and are used solely to upload and publish the operator's videos through each platform's official content-publishing API (TikTok's Content Posting API; Meta's Instagram Graph API). They are never transmitted to any party other than the platform that issued them.

3. Information sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share any information with third parties.

4. Cookies and tracking

This website is a static informational page. It sets no cookies and uses no analytics or tracking technologies.

5. Data deletion

Authorization can be revoked at any time from the platform's own settings — TikTok: Settings → Security & permissions → Apps and services; Instagram: Settings → Apps and websites — after which the stored tokens cease to function; locally stored credentials are deleted by the operator on request or on revocation. Contact the address below for any deletion request.
